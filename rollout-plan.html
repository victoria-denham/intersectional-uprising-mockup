every chapter can use.

01 The situation

The brand is the bottleneck.

Right now, every chapter produces content their own way. Some posts are bold and effective. Others look like they came from a different organisation entirely. The feed has no visual thread. The mark is not being used. New chapters have nothing to start from. That is not a content problem. It is a brand infrastructure problem.

The symptom

15 chapters, 15 different visual languages

Scroll IU's Instagram and you will see posts in orange, pink, white, black and purple with different fonts, different layouts and different tones. Some are partner content posted as-is. Some are hand-made with whatever app was nearest. None of it adds up to a brand.

The cause

No system, no templates, no mark anyone uses

The current logo does not translate to graphic use. There is no agreed colour palette, no shared fonts, no templates. Every chapter social lead is starting from scratch. Every post is a new design decision, and most of those decisions look different from everyone else's.

The risk

14K followers, diluted by inconsistency

An audience that size is a genuine asset. But if the feed does not read as a coherent movement, that audience does not compound. New followers do not stay because there is no strong identity to attach to. Partners and press do not take it seriously because it does not look serious.

The opportunity

You do not need a rebrand. You need a first brand.

Nothing has to change about what IU is. The energy, the causes, the community stay exactly as they are. What changes is that everything starts looking like it came from the same place. One mark. One palette. One system. Built for volunteers, not designers.

03 How it reaches the chapters

One folder. Shared once.
Used everywhere.

01

I build and zip the package

All assets: mark files, Canva templates, the one-page guide and the AI prompt are compiled into a single downloadable folder. Everything is named clearly. Nothing requires a designer to open it.

02

You share it with chapter leads

One email, one link, one zip. Each chapter social lead downloads the folder. The link works for every new chapter that joins after that too. Or share templates via a Canva team folder so everything updates from one place.

03

Chapter leads open it in Canva

Upload the templates to your Canva account. All nine Instagram templates are there. Open one, drag in the photo, type the text, export. No design decisions to make. The system makes them for you.

04

Use the AI prompt for copy

Paste the brand prompt into Claude or ChatGPT. Ask it to write a caption, a script for a story or a press release for a local paper. It knows the brand voice, the values and the tone. It writes like IU, not like a generic charity.

05

The feed becomes coherent

Every chapter posts in the same visual language. Different cities, different content, different photos. But the same mark, the same palette, the same type. For the first time, scrolling IU's feed feels like one movement.
