Who we are

Liberation is collective.

Intersectional Uprising is a grassroots activist collective united in the fight for women's rights. We operate through demonstration, education, and community engagement to advance equity and justice.

We centre marginalised communities: women in Afghanistan, Iran, Palestine, and the United States. Trans women, women of colour, disabled women, migrants, and working-class women. We recognise that overlapping forms of oppression cannot be addressed one at a time.

Intersectionality Feminism must be intersectional to be meaningful.
Inclusive spaces All genders welcome, united by shared commitment.
Accountability Zero tolerance for discrimination or hate.
Collective action Because liberation is never individual.

The movement

One movement. Fourteen cities. Marching together.

This is not a London organisation with regional outposts. Every chapter is locally led, locally rooted, and nationally connected. When we march, we march everywhere at once.

Support us

Every contribution matters.

We are an entirely volunteer-run organisation. Every donation, big or small, helps us keep marching, keep organising, and keep fighting.
