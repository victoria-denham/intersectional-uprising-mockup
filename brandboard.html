03 — Typography

Display / Hero — Cormorant Garamond Italic

Liberation is collective.

Used for hero statements, pull quotes, and section openers. Brings the editorial broadsheet and suffragette heritage into the layout.

Impact / Sections — Bebas Neue

WE MARCH.

Used for section headers, nav labels, call-to-action buttons, and any bold protest-poster moments in the layout.

Subheading — Cormorant Garamond Small Caps

Twice a Year. Nationwide.

Bridges the two display styles. Works for card headings, team names, section subtitles.
