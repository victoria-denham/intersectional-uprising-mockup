What a consistent grid looks like.

1
Campaign statement Static post

Mark + Bebas Neue headline on deep violet. Used for solidarity statements, campaign launches, or key moments. Change the headline text only — everything else is fixed in the template.

Example caption
Women's rights are human rights. Full stop.

We march because the rights we thought were settled are being stripped back. In the UK. In the US. In Iran. Everywhere.

If it's not intersectional, it's not feminism.

#IntersectionalUprising #WomensRights #Feminism
2, 5, 8
Chapter photography Static post

Real march photos, consistent colour grade, city label. Drag your photo into the template — filter and overlay are locked so every chapter looks like it belongs on the same feed.

Example caption
Manchester showed up. 🖤

Hundreds of us on the streets last Saturday. This is what a movement looks like.

Find your local chapter at the link in bio.

#Manchester #IntersectionalUprising #WomensMarch
3
Quote / principle Static post

Cormorant italic on parchment. The quieter tile — used for founding principles, powerful quotes, or a single line that needs space around it. Change the quote and attribution only.

Example caption
This is the principle we organise around. Not as a slogan — as a practice.

It means we don't march for some women. We march for all of us.

#IntersectionalFeminism #SolidarityNotSelectivity
4
Event countdown Static post

Pink background, large number, event name. Post this once a week in the lead-up to a march. Change the number and event name each time — nothing else moves.

Example caption
28 days. We're marching in 15 cities on the same day.

Find your nearest chapter and register at the link in bio. Bring someone who's never been before.

#UKWomensMarch #IntersectionalUprising
6
Chapter spotlight Carousel cover

This is the cover slide of a carousel post about a specific chapter. Slide 1 is this green tile (change city name only). Slides 2–4 are photos from that chapter's marches and events, posted by the national account to amplify local chapters.

Example caption
Chapter spotlight: Derby. One of our most active chapters in the country — and they've only been going 18 months.

Swipe to see what they've built. Want to join them? Link in bio.

#Derby #IntersectionalUprising #ChapterSpotlight
7
Statistic Static post

Near-black background, large pink number. For impact data — cities, marchers, chapters, signatures. Change the number and the label. Use sparingly so it retains weight when it appears.

Example caption
15 cities. One day. Thousands of us.

That's what happened in October. In March we're doing it again — bigger.

Every chapter that marches is proof this movement is growing. Find yours at the link in bio.

#15Cities #IntersectionalUprising
9
Call to action Pinned post

Ochre. This tile doesn't change. It lives at position 9 (bottom right) and is pinned as a post on the profile. Always the same message: find your chapter, join the uprising. It's the constant anchor of the grid.

Caption — stays the same always
Not sure where to start? Find a chapter near you and join us.

We have chapters in 15 cities across the UK. All genders welcome. No experience needed. Just show up.

Link in bio — intersectionaluprising.co.uk

#JoinUs #IntersectionalUprising #FindYourChapter
